About Us

Our small farm is the perfect destination for families. Located in the outskirts of Berlin, we offer a small oasis of nature. Stroll around among our fields, admire and feed farm animals, buy plants for your home and garden as well as our ecological products and enjoy a cup of coffee or tea in our coffee shop.
